Binary Bracelets

March 10, 2025

Our members helped make Binary Bracelets, a creative intersection between computer science and fundraising! Using colored beads, each bracelet represents sequences of zeros and ones, encoding short messages. One color stands for zeros, another for ones, and a third serves as a base color.
